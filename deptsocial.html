DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL WORK

The Department of Social Work had its humble beginning in 1955 with a Diploma Programme in social services (DSS). Later in 1961 the school started a Master of Social Work (MSW) Programme with specialisations in Personnel Management / Labour Welfare and Industrial Relations. In the year 1969 more specialisations such as Social Welfare Administration, Family and Child Welfare, Community Development and Medical and Psychiatric Social Work were introduced in the MSW programme. The MSW Programme of the school earned a wide acclaim in a short span of time. The School also expanded by taking up social work projects by establishing different Live Labs, and Research and Training in Social Work and related fields. Internationalisation of the Social Work Education (student and faculty exchange programmes) were started in the year 1998 with the signing of the official MOU with the Western Michigan University, USA and Rajagiri College of Social Sciences. The Department is the first in India to offer varied levels of Social Work education starting from one-year diploma to Doctoral levels (DSS, BSW, MSW, MPhil -Social Work, and Ph.D.). In 2013, the department started second batch of aided MSW programme and in the year 2019, Social Work in CSR has been introduced as a new specialisation to the MSW programme. The Department is rated as the second best in social work education in the country.
